\begin{abstract}
Number of marriages in Toronto has been barely changing for last 10
years. However, starting in 2020, pattern of marriages has been
dramatically changed due to pandemic. In this paper, it can show that
pattern of marriages licenses issued during pandemic to current time
(2020-2024) converted to random cloud. This implies that pandemic ruined
the marriage pattern in Toronto.
\end{abstract}
